- Open Browser and connect to "10.42.0.1:5000".

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/2.AI/after_ai_edu_0.png


Reactivating lcd monitor
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- First, click on "zetabankhub/edu:melodic-lcd-" to reactivate the screen display container.

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/2.AI/after_ai_edu_1_start_lcd.png

- If the container is in STOPPED state, press start and wait for a while.

- The "zetabankhub/edu:melodic-lcd-" text turns green when the container is starting up.

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/2.AI/after_ai_edu_2_start_lcd.png

- The screen display is now activated and visible.

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/2.AI/after_ai_edu_3_start_lcd_result.jpg



Reactivating camera
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Next, click on "zetabankhub/edu:melodic-l4t-csi-" to reactivate the camera container as well.

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/2.AI/after_ai_edu_4_start_csi.png

- If the container is in STOPPED state, press start and wait for a while.

- The "zetabankhub/edu:melodic-l4t-csi-" text turns green when the container is starting up.

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/2.AI/after_ai_edu_5_start_csi.png

- Now, your camera and screen display are ready to use again for other purposes.

- Remember, ensure that your AI training is fully completed before reactivating the camera and the screen display.
