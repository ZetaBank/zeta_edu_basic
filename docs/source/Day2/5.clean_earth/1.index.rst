Marine Debris
^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Marine debris is making the island dirty and polluting the environment.
- Marine debris, which consists of various types of waste materials such as plastics, fishing nets, bottles, and other discarded items, poses a grave threat to the fragile island ecosystems.

.. thumbnail:: /_images/Day2/5.clean_earth/clean_earth1.png

- The adults of Ulleungdo actively act to protect and preserve the environment of the beautiful island.
- Recognizing the importance of their surroundings, they are cleaning up the trash to ensure the sustainability and natural beauty of Ulleungdo for future generations.
